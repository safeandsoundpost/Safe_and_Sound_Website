## The Project

Trapped is a short film directed by Paul Persic and produced by Letter50 Films, starring Liam Leone, Alexandra Floras-Matic, Siddharth Sharma, and Marcella Young.

From the first cut, it was clear this film would live or die by its sound. The story is intimate and tense — and that called for a sound design approach that was equally restrained and precise.

## Our Approach

We worked closely with Paul to build a sonic world that felt claustrophobic without being overwhelming. Every ambient layer was chosen deliberately — the hum of a room, the weight of silence, the texture of breath.

Kyle Blaseg composed the original score, weaving between moments of stillness and tension in a way that complemented the performances without overshadowing them.
